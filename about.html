About Me

My Career Journey

Background

Before entering the UI/UX field, I worked as a lead website UI designer at a marketing agency, by creating concepts from initial through final design, delivering results for clients. As a team player, I thrive when collaborating and working on projects with groups. I was interested in understanding how designing a user interface can affect user behaviors. I learned more about creating functional design rather than just beautiful design. While working I then enrolled into the Udacity Nanodegree UX Design and intro to programming program to fine-tune my skills and transition to UX Design.

Skills & Experience

Since beginning my journey as a freelance web designer & UX/UI, I’ve done remote work for agencies and collaborated with talented people to create web products for both business and user, I designing digital experiences and building websites animation and design tools & coding HTML/CSS/JS/ Wordpress, and I enjoy working through all phases of the UX design process that includes research, ideation, wireframes, prototypes, visual design, and usability testing.
